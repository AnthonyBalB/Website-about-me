How Did I Get Where I am Now?

I was born in Arcadia, California around 2003. During my time in California, I only lived there up until I was 4 or 5 which was when I moved so I can't really remember my time there.

So, for the next ten years of my life I lived in a small state called Missouri. During this time my dad worked as a doctor in Bloomsdale, and I went to a school called St.Agnes elementary. St.Agnes was a small school and it didn't have a lot of popularity for the private school it was. Around this time I got a dog and later a cat. It would not be until I finished 6th grade that my dad got a call to help work in California, which was mainly where most of my relatives lived.

When we moved to California, I was going to start 7th grade at a school called St.Dorothys. St.Dorothys was also a private school, but it's also the place where the Oak Tree festival would be held. This would also be another place where I got my fish. that is still alive. A lot of things had happened at St.Dorothys, but it all leads to when I chose to go to Damien which is where I am now as a sophmore.
